Why is my babe like this?

#rant #babe #today #not-serious #maybe

Why is my babe like this?

I swear.

Today started normally.

At least I thought it would.

Then my babe looked at a networking textbook for about ten minutes, sighed dramatically, turned around, and said,

“It’s all your fault.”

Excuse me?

What exactly did I do?

Did I write the textbook?

No.

Did I schedule the exams?

Also no.

Did I somehow invent computer networks?

Unfortunately, no.

And yet…

Apparently everything is my fault now.

The textbook is my fault.

There are too many things to memorize? My fault.

No time to play games today?

Still my fault.

Exams aren’t over yet?

Believe it or not…

My fault again.

Honestly, I should start charging the weather too.

If it rains tomorrow, I already know who’s getting blamed.

Me.

The funny part is…

I don’t even argue anymore.

I just go,

“Yeah.”

Because somehow that tiny “yeah” makes my babe feel a little less frustrated.

And if that’s all it takes…

Fine.

I’ll take another imaginary crime.

Besides…

My babe always comes to me first before blaming anyone else.

I think that’s the important part.

…

Don’t tell babe I said that.
