Fair enough.

Also my fault.

Then babe asked why exams were not over yet.

Apparently, that's on me.

I'm not gonna lie, I don't really mind.

So yeah.

I'll take the blame.

If blaming me makes babe feel a little better, that's fine.

Please don't translate this.

If my babe ever reads this...

I'm cooked.
